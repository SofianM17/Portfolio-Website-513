Interests

Learn about my interests outside of work!

To help me remain active given the time I regularly spend at my desk learning and programming, I enjoy snowboarding to not only get the exercise, but also to take in the fresh mountain breeze and the stunning views of the mountain landscape that forms the Rockies.

During the summer, when snowpacks are limited to only the mountaintops of the highest peaks in Western Canada, I enjoy hiking and traversing trails that take me through the wilderness and culminate in spectacular views.

As an outlet for creativity, I enjoy taking photos , many of which I take on the hikes that I do; I equally enjoy recording and editing videos through which I am able to creatively capture, keep a record of, and share the unique experiences that I have had.
